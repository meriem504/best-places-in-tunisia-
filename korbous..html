Korbous

Korbous is a town located in the northeastern part of Tunisia, a country in North Africa. It is situated on the coast of the Mediterranean Sea, approximately 60 kilometers southeast of the capital, Tunis. Korbous is known for its natural hot springs and is a popular destination for tourists seeking relaxation and therapeutic treatments. The town is surrounded by picturesque landscapes, including hills and cliffs overlooking the sea.
